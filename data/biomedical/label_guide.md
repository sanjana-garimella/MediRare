## What Counts As a Misdiagnosis Trail?

A **misdiagnosis trail** is the sequence of diagnoses a patient actually received **before** the correct diagnosis.

Do NOT include:
- a list of possible diagnoses a clinician considered (differential diagnosis)
- diseases mentioned only as background
